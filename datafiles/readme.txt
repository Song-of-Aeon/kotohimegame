This is reading game.

Explore the adventures of The Little Rabbit Princess to reach the ending.

You cannot save your game and continue where you left off (sorry).


Controls -

Z or Enter or or S or Click to advance text

X or A or Esc to hide textbox

Ctrl to fast-forward

Alt + F4 to exit game

Made for the Touhou Pride Jam 4
